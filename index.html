Our Services

We provide end-to-end garments sourcing services, acting as your extended office on the ground in Bangladesh.

Product Sourcing & Development

Trend-aligned product ideas, fabric and trims sourcing, sample development, fit refinement, and competitive costing aligned with your target price points.

Factory Screening & Compliance

Comprehensive evaluation of factory capabilities, production capacity, technical strength, and operational standards — including full assessment of social and environmental compliance frameworks such as BSCI, SEDEX, WRAP, OEKO-TEX, and other globally recognized certifications.

Production Planning & Follow-Up

Time & Action (T&A) planning, production tracking, raw material follow-up, and risk management with regular status updates to buyers.

Quality Assurance & Inspections

Structured inline inspections, pre-final and final AQL checks, measurement verification, and detailed inspection reports with photos.

Logistics & Documentation

Coordination with nominated forwarders, packing list and invoice review, and shipment follow-up through ETD/ETA for a smoother logistics experience.

Sustainability & Ethical Sourcing

Encouraging cleaner production, supporting factories on audits and improvement plans, and promoting ethical and responsible manufacturing across the supply base.

How We Work

A simple, transparent, and structured process from idea to shipment ensures better planning, fewer surprises, and smoother execution.

1

Requirement Sharing

Buyer shares tech packs, target prices, and delivery timelines for the collection or program.

2

Product Design & Development

Our design team creates technical specifications, patterns, and prototypes based on initial requirements.

3

Factory & Fabric Matching

We shortlist suitable factories and fabric sources based on product type, volume, and compliance needs.

4

Sampling & Approvals

Samples are developed, fits are refined, and final approvals are obtained before order confirmation.

5

Production & Quality Control

Daily follow-up with inline and final AQL checks, along with measurement and finishing control.

6

Shipment & Documentation

Coordination with nominated forwarders, document review, and shipment status updates until dispatch.

Our Factory Network

BGS partners with a curated network of woven, knitwear, denim, sweater, and workwear factories across key production hubs in Bangladesh.

BGS works with an extensive and carefully vetted network of 50+ factories across Bangladesh, covering every major product category — knitwear, woven apparel, sweaters, sportswear, workwear, denim, and accessories. This wide manufacturing ecosystem allows us to support small, medium, and large-scale orders with reliability, flexibility, and consistent quality.
Our partner factories are located across key production hubs such as Gazipur, Savar, Ashulia, Narayanganj, and Chattogram, ensuring diversified capabilities and strong production capacity throughout the year. Each unit is selected based on its technical strength, operational stability, and proven ability to deliver across varied fabric types, garment styles, and finishing requirements.
We treat compliance as a serious matter requirement. All sourcing partners are assessed for adherence to internationally recognized standards, and we prioritize factories following BSCI, SEDEX, WRAP, OEKO-TEX, and RSC/ACCORD frameworks. This approach ensures ethical production, safe working environments, and full alignment with the expectations of global brands.
With this strong, scalable network, BGS ensures buyers receive dependable manufacturing support tailored to their volume, quality, and compliance needs — from basic essentials to high-value specialty garments.

Compliance and certifications (such as BSCI, SEDEX, WRAP, OEKO-TEX, RSC/ACCORD) are considered based on buyer requirements and factory profiles.

Sustainability & Ethical Sourcing

We believe long-term success is only possible when products are made responsibly, respecting people, communities, and the environment.

People & Workplace

We prioritise factories with safe, fair, and respectful working conditions, and encourage continuous improvement in worker welfare and social compliance.

Process & Environment

Cleaner production, responsible use of chemicals, and reduced environmental impact are key focus areas we encourage within our factory network.

Long-Term Responsibility

Our sustainability approach is practical and improvement-oriented. We support factories with audit preparation, corrective action plans, and long-term capability building rather than short-term fixes.

We believe that good business is sustainable business — and we strive to align sourcing decisions with that belief.
