Printing & Customization

We offer various printing and customization options to make your knitwear products unique.

Screen Printing

Traditional printing method ideal for bulk orders with simple designs and limited colors.

DTG Printing

Direct-to-garment printing for complex, full-color designs with photographic quality.

Sublimation

Permanent, full-color printing that becomes part of the fabric, ideal for polyester garments.

Heat Transfer

Versatile printing method using pre-printed transfers applied with heat and pressure.
