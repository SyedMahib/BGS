Our AQL Standards

Quality assurance is one of our highest priorities. We follow internationally accepted AQL (Acceptable Quality Limit) inspection standards to maintain consistent product quality across all orders:

Critical Defects Zero Tolerance
Major Defects ≤ 2.5%
Minor Defects ≤ 4.0%

We also fully respect and implement any buyer-specific quality standards, inspection protocols, or compliance requirements.

Inspection Reports Include:

Our experienced QA team performs product inspections based on internationally accepted quality standards and industry best practices, aligned with leading inspection bodies such as SGS, Intertek, Bureau Veritas (BV), and similar organizations.

We can conduct inspections on behalf of our customers and share comprehensive inspection reports to ensure confidence, transparency, and shipment readiness.

At the same time, we welcome and fully cooperate with any buyer-nominated third-party inspection agency.

Our Service Process

A structured approach that ensures transparency, quality, and timely delivery at every stage

1

Requirement Analysis

We begin by thoroughly understanding your product requirements, target market, quality expectations, and delivery timelines. This includes analysis of tech packs, samples, and any specific compliance needs.

2

Factory Matching & Costing

Based on your requirements, we identify suitable factories from our curated network. We obtain detailed cost breakdowns and present you with transparent pricing options that align with your budget.

3

Sampling & Development

We coordinate the development of proto samples, fit samples, and pre-production samples. Our team ensures samples meet your specifications before proceeding to bulk production.

4

Production Management

We monitor the entire production process with regular updates, inline quality checks, and proactive problem-solving. Our on-ground presence ensures smooth execution and timely delivery.

5

Quality Assurance

Before shipment, we conduct comprehensive final inspections following AQL standards. We provide detailed inspection reports with photos and measurements for your review and approval.

6

Shipment & Documentation

We coordinate with your nominated freight forwarders, prepare all necessary shipping documents, and ensure smooth customs clearance. You receive regular updates until your goods reach their destination.
