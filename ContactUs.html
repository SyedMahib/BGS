Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with us.

What information should I provide when contacting you?

For the most accurate response, please include: product type, target quantities, quality requirements, delivery timeline, and any technical specifications or tech packs you have.

How long does it take to get samples?

Sample development typically takes 2-3 weeks depending on complexity. This includes fabric sourcing, pattern making, and sample production. Rush samples may be possible for an additional fee.

Do you work with small businesses?

Yes! We work with businesses of all sizes. Our minimum order quantities start from 300 pieces for woven and knit products, making us accessible to growing brands and established companies alike.

Can you help with compliance and certifications?

Absolutely. We partner with factories that hold various certifications (BSCI, SEDEX, WRAP, OEKO-TEX) and can guide you through compliance requirements for your target markets.
